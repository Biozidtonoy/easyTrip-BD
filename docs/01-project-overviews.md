# Bangladesh Tourism Platform – Project Overview

## Executive Summary

Bangladesh Tourism Platform is a web-based travel management system designed to help travelers discover tourist destinations, explore accommodations, share travel experiences, and plan trips efficiently within Bangladesh. The platform centralizes tourism information into a single digital service, making travel planning easier while increasing the visibility of local tourist attractions and businesses.

---

## Project Background

Bangladesh has a rich collection of natural landscapes, historical landmarks, beaches, forests, and cultural heritage sites. However, tourism-related information is often scattered across multiple websites and social media platforms, making it difficult for travelers to find reliable and organized information.

Many travelers struggle to compare destinations, locate nearby accommodations, read authentic reviews, and organize travel plans from one place. This fragmented experience creates unnecessary effort and reduces confidence in travel decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Current tourism services face several challenges:

* Tourism information is distributed across multiple sources.
* Travelers have difficulty discovering new destinations.
* Hotel and accommodation information is inconsistent or outdated.
* Reviews and travel experiences are scattered across social media.
* There is no centralized platform for planning domestic travel.

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop a centralized tourism platform that provides:

* User registration and secure authentication.
* Destination discovery with detailed information.
* Hotel and accommodation listings.
* Booking management for accommodations.
* Traveler reviews and ratings.
* Search and filtering based on location and category.
* Personalized user profiles and favorite destinations.
* Responsive web application accessible across devices.

---

| Reviews & Ratings      | Users can submit reviews and rate destinations or hotels          |

---

## Assumptions

* Users have access to the internet and modern web browsers.
* Tourism information is regularly updated by administrators.
* Hotels maintain accurate accommodation and availability information.
* The initial release focuses only on tourism within Bangladesh.

---

## Constraints

| Constraint                    | Impact                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Fixed semester timeline       | MVP features must be prioritized                                   |
| Limited development resources | Scope is restricted to essential features                          |
| Limited budget                | Third-party services and integrations are minimized                |
| Data accuracy                 | Platform depends on administrators maintaining current information |

---

## Out of Scope

* International tourism services.
* Flight, train, or bus ticket booking.
* Native Android and iOS mobile applications.
* AI-powered travel recommendations in the initial release.
* Real-time chat between travelers and hotel owners.
