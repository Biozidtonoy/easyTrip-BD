# Bangladesh Tourism Platform – Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholder Categories

### Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder            | Role                              | Key Expectation                                                        |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Travelers              | Primary users of the platform     | Easy access to reliable tourism information and seamless trip planning |
| Hotel Owners           | List and manage accommodations    | Increased visibility, bookings, and customer engagement                |
| Platform Administrator | Manage platform content and users | Efficient content management and secure platform operations            |

---

### Secondary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder         | Role                                   | Key Expectation                                                  |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tourism Businesses  | Promote travel-related services        | Greater exposure to potential customers                          |
| Tourism Authorities | Support domestic tourism initiatives   | Accurate representation of tourist destinations and tourism data |
| Customer Support    | Assist users with inquiries and issues | Efficient issue resolution and user satisfaction                 |

---

### Internal Stakeholders

* Development Team
* Project Manager
* Quality Assurance (QA)
* UI/UX Designer
* Database Administrator
* System Administrator
* Security Team

---

### External Stakeholders

* Cloud Service Provider
* Payment Gateway Provider (Future Enhancement)
* Mapping and Location Service Providers
* University Supervisor and Course Instructor
* Local Tourism Organizations

## Stakeholder Responsibilities

| Stakeholder            | Primary Responsibility                                                       |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Travelers              | Search destinations, make bookings, submit reviews, and provide feedback     |
| Hotel Owners           | Maintain accommodation details, availability, and booking information        |
| Platform Administrator | Manage users, destinations, hotels, reviews, and overall platform operations |
| Development Team       | Design, develop, test, and maintain the application                          |
| QA Team                | Ensure software quality, usability, and reliability                          |
| Tourism Authorities    | Provide accurate tourism information and support promotional initiatives     |
| University Supervisor  | Review project progress and ensure academic requirements are met             |

---

## Stakeholder Communication Plan

| Stakeholder            | Communication Method                             | Frequency |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | --------- |
| Development Team       | Team meetings, GitHub Issues, Project Board      | Weekly    |
| Project Manager        | Progress reports and planning meetings           | Weekly    |
| University Supervisor  | Project demonstrations and documentation reviews | Bi-weekly |
| Platform Administrator | System reports and administrative dashboard      | As Needed |
| Hotel Owners           | Email notifications and platform updates         | As Needed |
| Travelers              | Website announcements and email notifications    | As Needed |

---

## Stakeholder Goals

* Deliver a reliable and user-friendly tourism platform.
* Increase the visibility of tourist destinations and accommodations across Bangladesh.
* Simplify travel planning through centralized and accurate information.
* Improve user satisfaction with secure booking and review features.
* Support the growth of domestic tourism by connecting travelers with tourism-related businesses.
