# Bangladesh Tourism Platform – Problem Statement

## Problem Context

Several stakeholder groups are affected by the lack of a centralized tourism information system in Bangladesh.

| Segment             | Typical Context                                                | Core Frictions                                                                      |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Travelers           | Planning vacations, family trips, and weekend tours            | Difficulty finding reliable information, comparing destinations, and planning trips |
| Hotel Owners        | Promoting accommodations and managing bookings                 | Limited online visibility and inconsistent customer reach                           |
| Tourism Businesses  | Advertising travel services and attractions                    | Fragmented promotion across multiple platforms                                      |
| Tourism Authorities | Promoting domestic tourism and maintaining tourism information | Difficulty providing accurate, up-to-date information through a single platform     |

---

## Current State (As-Is)

* Tourism information is scattered across websites, blogs, and social media platforms.
* Travelers spend significant time searching for destinations, accommodations, and travel recommendations.
* Hotel availability and pricing are often inconsistent or outdated.
* Reviews and travel experiences are spread across multiple sources, making them difficult to verify.
* There is no unified platform that integrates destination information, accommodations, reviews, and trip planning.

---

## Future State (To-Be)

* A centralized tourism platform providing reliable and up-to-date destination information.
* Organized hotel listings with detailed descriptions and booking functionality.
* Verified traveler reviews and ratings to support informed decision-making.
* Powerful search and filtering options to quickly discover destinations and accommodations.
* A secure, user-friendly platform that simplifies domestic travel planning.

---

## Business Impact

| Impact Area             | Current Cost                                        | Expected Benefit                                          |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Travel Planning         | Time-consuming research across multiple sources     | Faster and more efficient trip planning                   |
| Tourism Promotion       | Limited visibility for destinations and businesses  | Increased exposure for tourist attractions and hotels     |
| Booking Process         | Manual communication and fragmented booking methods | Simplified online booking experience                      |
| User Confidence         | Inconsistent and unreliable tourism information     | Greater trust through centralized and verified content    |
| Domestic Tourism Growth | Reduced awareness of many tourist destinations      | Increased tourism participation and local economic growth |

---

## Problem Statement

Travelers in Bangladesh lack a centralized, reliable, and user-friendly platform for discovering tourist destinations, comparing accommodations, reading authentic travel experiences, and planning trips. The current reliance on scattered websites and social media results in inefficient planning, inconsistent information, and reduced confidence in travel decisions. The Bangladesh Tourism Platform addresses these challenges by providing a secure, centralized web application that connects travelers with destinations, accommodations, reviews, and booking services, creating a more efficient and accessible tourism ecosystem.
